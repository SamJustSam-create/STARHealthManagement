Who We Are

Training That Prepares You
When It Matters Most

STAR Health is a nationally registered training provider delivering practical, scenario-based first aid, fire safety, and emergency response courses across Australia. Whether you're an individual looking to build confidence in a crisis, or an organisation seeking to meet compliance requirements, our programs are built around real-world application.

Our instructors bring hands-on experience from clinical and operational environments — ensuring every participant leaves with skills they can actually use.
